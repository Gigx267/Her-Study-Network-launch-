About Her Study Network

Her Study Network is an elegant sanctuary where women gather to study Scripture, pray with intention, and bring faith into daily life. Our story is built on love in action and hope in every gift.

Become a Member Connect with Us
🕊️

Our Vision

To cultivate a global sisterhood rooted in prayer, study, and marketplace influence — transforming society through grace and excellence.

📖

Our Mission

Equip women to live faith boldly, intercede fervently, and steward their professional and creative callings for Kingdom impact.

🤝

Core Identity

A sacred community where devotion meets dedication, and every woman finds a place to belong, grow, and release her purpose.

Where faith & purpose intersect

Her Study Network began as a whisper from the prayer closet — a divine call to create a sanctuary where women could deepen their study of Scripture, engage in persistent intercession, and practically demonstrate the love of Christ.

HSN is not just another gathering; it is a movement of women committed to radical consecration and actionable compassion. Through weekly prayer calls, Bible immersions, and outreach initiatives, we are reshaping communities from the inside out.

As we connect faith to daily life, we produce women who become hope carriers, using their resources, skills, and time to lift the vulnerable. Love in action, hope in every gift is our blueprint.

Rooted in Three Pillars

🔥 Persistent Prayer

A prayer platform for women — weekly intercessory calls, prophetic declarations for families, cities, and nations. We pray until something shifts.

📚 Word & Wisdom

Deep, systematic study of the Bible and relevant teachings that empower women to navigate modern life with ancient truth.

🤲 Radical Outreach

Love in action. Our quarterly outreaches provide food, clothes, school supplies and hope to underserved communities, reflecting Jesus’s heart.

"This platform belongs to all of us."

HSN is a shared space — a spiritual home built on trust, mutual edification, and the desire to see every woman rise. Our leadership team consists of marketplace ministers, prayer captains, and creative directors who sacrifice to keep this vision alive.
